ABOUT


Reginald "Reggie" Telemaque is a highly motivated and focused driven student-athlete at the University of Saint Francis. Reggie is majoring in Computer Information systems and intends to land somewhere in the information technology field in either hardware or software-based career.
At the University of Saint Francis, Reggie is currently employed as a Social Media Assistant where he makes graphics for the football team and runs and manages our social media accounts. works in Adobe Creative Cloud programs, Gimp, and many other design and management programs. As well as in his studies working in C#, Javascript, and HTML. Reggie is also a part-time fulfillment associate at Lowes Home Improvement. He hopes to apply the skills he obtains and experiences that will help him in any environment. in ways that will not just help not just himself but the company he works for and the people around him.
Message from Reggie: I pride myself on an education first mentality. being a student-athlete wasn't always easy. In this time I learned time management, leadership, scholarship, reliability, and that every day is a grind and nothing is handed to you. That you must work harder every single day to become the best you can be and never give up!
